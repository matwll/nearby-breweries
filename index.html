Beer day in America

National Beer Day is celebrated yearly in the United States on the 7th of April. The economy benefits from beer sales.

Consumed drink

- Beer is the third most-consumed drink in the world, after water and tea.
- When it comes to total beer consumption, China ranks number one.

What is brewing?

Brewing is the production of beer by steeping a starch source in water and fermenting the resulting sweet liquid with yeast. It may be done in a brewery by a commercial brewer, at home by a homebrewer, or communally.

Beer Producing

- China, the United States, Germany, Russia and Brazil are among the top five beer-producing countries.
- More than 7,000 breweries manufacture beers in the United States.
- California is the biggest producer of beer in the U.S. with over 1,100 breweries
